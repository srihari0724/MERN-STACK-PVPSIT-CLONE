Admissions at PVPSIT

We are excited to welcome new students to PVPSIT. Our admission process is simple and transparent. Please review the guidelines and apply online through the official portal.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Undergraduate Courses: A minimum of 50% in the 12th grade with Science as the main stream (for Engineering courses).
  • Postgraduate Courses: A valid B.Tech degree in the respective discipline with a minimum of 60% marks.

Admission Process

  • Complete the online application form.
  • Submit required documents (transcripts, ID proof, etc.).
  • Appear for the entrance exam (if applicable).
  • Attend the counseling session.
  • Confirm your seat by paying the required fees.

Important Dates

  • Application Start Date: 1st March 2024
  • Application Deadline: 30th June 2024
  • Entrance Exam Date: 15th July 2024
