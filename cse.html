B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering offers programs focused on software development, AI, and cutting-edge technologies.

Introduction

Core Focus: The CSE department focuses on the theoretical and practical aspects of computing, software development, and system design. Relevance: Plays a vital role in shaping the digital world, influencing almost every industry. Key Areas of Study Programming and Software Development: Languages: Python, Java, C++, etc. Software engineering principles, algorithms, and data structures. Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning: Building intelligent systems, neural networks, and data-driven solutions. Data Science and Big Data: Handling and analyzing massive datasets for insights and decision-making.
