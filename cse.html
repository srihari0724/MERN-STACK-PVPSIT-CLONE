B.Tech in Computer Science and Engineering

The Department of Computer Science and Engineering offers programs focused on software development, AI, and cutting-edge technologies.

Introduction

The CSE department focuses on the theoretical and practical aspects of computing, software development, and system design. It plays a vital role in shaping the digital world, influencing almost every industry. Key areas of study include:

Key Areas of Study:

  • Programming and Software Development:
    • Languages: Python, Java, C++, etc.
    • Software engineering principles, algorithms, and data structures.
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning:
    • Building intelligent systems, neural networks, and data-driven solutions.
  • Data Science and Big Data:
    • Handling and analyzing massive datasets for insights and decision-making.
