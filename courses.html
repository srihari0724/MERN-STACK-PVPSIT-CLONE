Our Courses

At PVPSIT, we offer a wide range of courses across multiple disciplines. Our programs are designed to provide students with the skills and knowledge necessary to succeed in their chosen careers.

Undergraduate Programs

  • B.Tech Computer Science and Engineering: A comprehensive program that prepares students for a career in the rapidly evolving tech industry.
  • B.Tech Information Technology: A program focusing on IT infrastructure, software development, and network management.
  • B.Tech Electronics and Communication Engineering: Training students in designing and developing systems and devices for communication.
  • B.Tech Electrical and Electronics Engineering: A program that covers power generation, transmission, and electronics systems.
  • B.Tech Mechanical Engineering: Students gain expertise in designing and manufacturing mechanical systems.
  • B.Tech Civil Engineering: Training in construction, infrastructure development, and environmental engineering.

Postgraduate Programs

  • M.Tech in Computer Science: A deeper understanding of algorithms, software engineering, and IT systems.
  • M.Tech in VLSI Design: Specialized training in designing integrated circuits and systems for modern electronics.
